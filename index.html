About me. I am a Ph.D student in computer science at Max Planck Institute for Informatics, advised by Prof. Anja Feldmann and Prof. Balakrishnan Chandrasekaran.

Research Interests. I am broadly interested in systems and networking. Currently, my research focuses on cost-efficient video streaming and partially reliable transport.

Prior to MPI. I got my B.S. degree in the joint program between Queen Mary University of London and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2020.
